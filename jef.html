Love is a set of emotions, behaviors, and beliefs with strong
feelings of affection. So, for example,
a person might say he or she loves his or her dog, loves
freedom, or loves God.
The concept of love may become an unimaginable thing and
also it may happen to each person in a particular way. Love
has a variety of feelings, emotions, and attitude.
For someone love is more than just being interested
physically in another one, rather it is an emotional attachment.
We can say love is more of a feeling that a person feels for
another person. Therefore, the basic meaning of love is to feel
more than liking towards someone
